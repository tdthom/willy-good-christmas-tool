Willy Good Christmas Lights Tool

One app, four screens: Clients • Estimate • Photo Mockup • Proposal

Clients

This is where you’ll list customers and pick which job you’re working on.

Estimate

This is where you’ll enter roof/house measurements and see the price.

Photo Mockup

This is where you’ll upload a photo and mark where lights go.

Proposal / PDF

This is where you’ll show a clean proposal with your logo and total price.
